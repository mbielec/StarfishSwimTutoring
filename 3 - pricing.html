Pricing

Your first lesson with Starfish Swim Tutoring is FREE. I want to make sure that the service is right for you before you commit. After that, the cost is calculated based on lesson time.

$45/HOUR
$34/45 MINS
$23/HALF HOUR

When you purchase a class, you purchase the time. You can put from 1 to 3 people in each class, provided they are around the same level. For example, you can choose to have your kids take lessons together, or invite your friends to join the class in order to lower your cost.

I recommend that younger swimmers take private lessons, since they will have to take turns being in the water. What was a 30 minute lesson for 3 kids only amounts to 10 minutes swimming time for each one.
